Clarity Clinics

Clarity Clinics is our comprehensive healthcare management solution designed to streamline operations, enhance patient care, and improve efficiency in medical practices. Our software handles everything from patient records and appointments to billing and inventory management.

Key Features

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) management
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Billing and insurance claim processing
  • Pharmacy and inventory management
  • Patient portal for access to records
  • Reporting and analytics dashboard
  • Multi-location support
  • Secure data encryption and backup

Clarity Pharmacy

Clarity Pharmacy is a specialized management system designed for pharmacies of all sizes. It streamlines inventory management, prescription processing, and customer relations, helping you provide excellent service while maximizing efficiency.

Key Features

  • Inventory management with expiry tracking
  • Prescription processing and labeling
  • Supplier and purchase order management
  • Patient history and allergy alerts
  • Insurance and NHIS billing integration
  • Sales reporting and analytics
  • Multi-branch support
  • Mobile accessibility
Contact via WhatsApp

Clarity Retail

Clarity Retail is a comprehensive Point of Sale (POS) and inventory management solution designed for retail businesses. From small shops to multi-store chains, our software helps you manage sales, inventory, customers, and employees with ease.

Key Features

  • Point of Sale with multiple payment options
  • Inventory management with stock alerts
  • Customer relationship management (CRM)
  • Sales reporting and analytics
  • Employee management and commission tracking
  • Multi-store support
  • Barcode and label printing
  • Integration with accounting software
